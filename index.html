The problem

Your clients hired you for results. They spend 10 minutes staring at a 40-row spreadsheet instead.

Your media buying is world-class. Your reporting makes it invisible. Clients see a spreadsheet, not the 3x ROAS you delivered. The agency down the street sends a polished one-pager and looks like the better partner.

01

You pull data from Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, and TikTok Business Center into a spreadsheet. You add a tab for the LTV/CAC bridge, format the ROAS column, color-code the underperformers. It takes 3 hours. Your client opens it, scrolls to ROAS, and asks if that number is good. They did not read the rest.

02

Tools like AgencyAnalytics and Databox generate dashboards your clients can log into. They do not. Your clients are DTC operators — they have 14 tabs open and a warehouse shipment to track. What they need is a 60-second read that tells them what happened, what worked, and what you are changing next. Not a dashboard. A brief.

03

You are being measured on results, but you are presenting data. There is a difference. The agency that wins the renewal is the one whose report feels like it came from a strategist, not a spreadsheet jockey. Every agency your client compares you to sends the same AgencyAnalytics export.

How it works

Connect your ad accounts. AdPulse writes the executive summary.

Step 1

Connect Meta, Google, and TikTok in 60 seconds

AdPulse connects directly to your ad accounts via API. No manual CSV exports, no copy-pasting from three platforms. Your accounts stay connected and data refreshes automatically each month.

Step 2

AI writes the executive summary your client wants

AdPulse analyzes your campaign performance and writes a 3-paragraph executive summary in plain English — what hit target, what missed, why, and what you are adjusting next week. Your strategic framing, without the 3-hour writing block.

Step 3

Send a branded PDF that makes you look like a bigger agency

Every month, AdPulse generates a clean, branded one-pager with your agency logo — the executive summary up front, supporting data behind it. Your client gets a report they can read in 60 seconds. You spend that time on strategy, not formatting.

The time problem

Reclaim 3 hours every reporting week.

Manual reporting across Meta, Google, and TikTok takes 2-4 hours per client per month. With 5-10 clients, that is a week of non-billable time every month. AdPulse handles the synthesis so you can focus on campaign strategy.

Reports your clients remember are reports that renew.

Agency churn at the boutique level is almost never about performance — it is about perceived value. A DTC operator who reads your executive summary in 60 seconds and understands what you did and why retains you. One who stares at a spreadsheet does not.
